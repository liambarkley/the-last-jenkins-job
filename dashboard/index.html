📖 What's Happening
Chapter 1: The Setup
Jenkins wakes up, checks the environment, and realises what it's been asked to do.
Chapter 2: Arming Itself
Jenkins installs Terraform, Helm, and the ArgoCD CLI. The tools of its own undoing.
Chapter 3: The Blueprint
Terraform plans a full GitOps platform — ingress, ArgoCD, Prometheus, Grafana.
Chapter 4: Building It
Terraform applies. Kubernetes namespaces are created. Helm charts are deployed. The kingdom is built.
Chapter 5: Checking The Work
Jenkins won't remove itself until the platform is healthy. It has standards.
Chapter 6: Passing The Torch
Platform config is pushed to Gitea. ArgoCD is registered and told where to look. From this point, GitOps is in control.
Chapter 7: Verification
One final check. Everything is syncing. Everything is healthy. Jenkins nods approvingly at its own obsolescence.
Chapter 8: The Exit
Jenkins writes a farewell message, schedules its own container removal, and quietly disappears. Long live GitOps.
